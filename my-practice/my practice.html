Fashion Magazines

The birth of magazines dates to the late 1600s, under Queen Elizabeth, and over time, fashion magazines have become an indispensable tool of this billion-dollar industry while bringing more value to readers. Then, under Princess Anne, the content of newspapers shifted to women-centered topics and publications in the form of books or pocketbooks. These publications pioneer a community of knowledgeable readers and have a basis for exchanging information with each other. But it does not stop there; fashion magazines continue to develop, not only making fashion more accessible to the community by updating the latest trends but also a place to reflect social issues and many aspects of the culture of life.
